Description

The innovative potential of smart contracts is profound. If widely adopted, these computable and self-executing smart contracts can significantly reduce transaction time and costs, enable autonomous machine-to-machine transactions, and fundamentally alter how countless industries will interact with their clients. Because of this potential, all professionals must strive to understand this rapidly changing technology, as well as the unique benefits that it can pose. This talk will define smart contracts, explain their current and future business applications, discuss how they may transform the world, and focus on concrete steps you can take to prepare for the potential impact on your career.
