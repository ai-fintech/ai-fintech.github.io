Description

Learn how to raise an ICO from experts in the cryptocurrency industry!! Token sales, better known as ICOs, are the biggest disruptor in the technology world this year. Panelists will share insights to answer the following questions:

  1. What actually is an ICO?
  2. The SEC recently issued guidelines, are ICOs legal?
  3. Can you really raise over $50 million in just minutes?
  4. How do you go about holding an ICO?
  5. What happens to your company after an ICO?
  6. What do prospective ICO investors buy?
  7. What kinds of companies are holding ICOs?
  8. Does an ICO replace an IPO?
  9. And — most importantly — should traditional VCs worry about being replaced?

Speaker(s)

Anastasiya Sharkova, Kamal Hubbard, Joseph Harrel, Marina Wolters
