Below is a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).

  1. Can I buy tickets at the door?

    No. You must register in advance for this event.

  2. Do I need prior engineering experience to attend?

    No. Although many of our presentations and tutorials are highly technical, our introductory sessions are geared to participants with no prior experience.

  3. Who will be in attendance?

    200+ Technical and Investment Professionals from around the World!

    Our participants include students, industry professionals, engineers, researchers, entrepreneurs, investors, and those just curious to learn what all the fuss is about.

  4. Do I need to download and install any software prior to attending?

    Yes. If you plan on following along with workshop tutorials, it is recommended that you pre-install recommended software ahead of time. Presenters may not take time to walk through the installation process.

  5. What if I need help with package installations?

    Attend our optional software download and install mentoring session Friday evening. We'll have some mentors available throughout the weekend to assist as well.

  6. Can I participate remotely?

    Nothing compares to attending our sessions in person, whith one on one access to interact with presenters, mentors, and peers. We will provide live streaming of the forum on the Accel.AI Youtube account.

  7. Can you share info about my company/product/event with your attendees?

    You may become an official sponsor of our event to have your company, product, or event featured on our marketing materials and during the event to our attendees.

    Learn more about Sponsorship Opportunities!

    We do not make announcements for non-related or unsponsored events or conferences at our gatherings. Distribution of flyers, printed media or other non related materials is not allowed at our events.

  8. What is your refund policy?

    We do not offer refunds at this time.

  9. Can I volunteer for discounted entry?

    We need volunteers to help make our forum amazing!

    We value your time and contributions - volunteering gains you free entry!

    Apply to volunteer here.

  10. Where can I park?

    We highly reccomend using public transportation when attending this event.

    If you must drive, there are several pay parking lots and street parking available near the venue. See top cheapest and closets garages here.
